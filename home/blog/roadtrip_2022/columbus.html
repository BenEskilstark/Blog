12/21/2021

Columbus, Ohio

Yet another big driving day.

Inspired by my cabin fever yesterday, I had us listening to the Muppet Treasure Island songs while we drove. I had forgotten how funny that movie was, especially the group of muppet rats on board the pirate ship who think they're on a cruise and are singing about the margaritas at the midnight buffet. Plus the Trump-voter muppets who sing about forcing people to walk the plank and when confronted go "just kidding!"

We made it to Columbus without losing too much time and hung out in my parents' designated quarantine zone in the basement. First shower and warm bed in 4 days.

12/22/2021

First non-driving day in a bit. Exchanged Christmas present with my parents on their front patio which was really nice. Many errands were accomplished including the all-important Piada's lunch date (Piada's is Chipotle but for Italian food -- genius).

Catherine and I also went and climbed at the Audoban Metro Park climbing wall in 35 degree weather. It was miserable, and in my machismo I insisted on leading the big arch as the very first climb. It was just as hard as I remembered it being in high school and combined with my numb fingers turned into a hangdogging grind. It felt good to be moving again though.

At night, my high school friend Colin came over and we all watched Christmas Vacation out back by the fire pit. The movie is still so great!
