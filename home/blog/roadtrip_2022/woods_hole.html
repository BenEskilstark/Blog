12/24/2021

Woods Hole, Massachusetts

Snowy arrival in Woods Hole! We got set up in the suite apartment at Catherine's parents' house on the harbor, so we had our own little kitchen set up and everything. Due to our Covid scare earlier, we had Christmas in the garage (the huge and stately Garage-mahal) with the tree and everything in there. And we also zoomed all the extended families where we each had our own computer despite being in the same room. After Christmas, we went to Martha's Vineyard -- a different experience in the winter. Courcelle has a cute little house there that's two stories but skinny. And we of course went to the Vineyard library and got many books. Back on the cape, Wallace took the truck out with me to try to diagnose the grinding sound that has stumped many mechanics before. We didn't figure out anything conclusive but it was still a fun exercise. During this whole time Catherine was working hard on her sewing projects. We went to Joann's on Christmas Eve and bought a hundred dollars of fabric and zippers and fleece that Catherine turned into many very professional-looking little bike bags and pencial holders and fanny packs and bill folds. Plus some admittedly not-so-professional looking fleece sweatpants. It was amusing coming up from dinner with the parents at like 9pm only for Catherine to go right back to the sewing machine!
