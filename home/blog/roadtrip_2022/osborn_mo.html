12/20/2021

Osborn, Missouri

The driving today started to put me over the edge. Three days of non-stop driving in a row is just too many. So when at 8pm I saw a sign for a chipotle I swerved us right on over there. I'm not sure where exactly this stop was, but masking seemed to start being a thing again, at least among the employees of the stores (the grocery store we stopped at outside Denver had probably 95% masks, but everywhere else since Vegas was more like 5%, including store employees). Not wearing a mask when the employees don't care either is one thing, but not wearing a mask when the employees are and don't have a choice whether to be there or not just seems downright evil. But I'm the outsider here so I do nothing.

We were supposed to spend this night at our friends' house in Kansas City but because of our covid scare yesterday we diverted to a campground outside Osborn about an hour north. But the friends still decided to come up and join us in the cold up there that evening! We made a fire and hot chocolate (with bourbon) and caught up with each other.

This was probably the coldest night yet.
